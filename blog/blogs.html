Understanding Ethical Hacking

In today's digital world, ethical hacking is essential for cybersecurity. Unlike malicious hackers, ethical hackers, or "white hats," are authorized to probe systems for vulnerabilities.

Organizations hire them to identify weaknesses and strengthen defenses against real threats. With the demand for ethical hackers rising, it's a rewarding career choice—consider certifications like CEH or OSCP.
